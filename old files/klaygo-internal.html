Here's everything you need to know about booking a cleaning service with Klaygo PH.

Internal Use Only — Do not share this link publicly. Customer version: klaygo-how-it-works.html
1

Fill in your information

Start by entering your name, contact number, email, complete address, and the nearest landmark. This helps us verify your location and send you a copy of your estimate.

Why do we need your email?Once you send your booking request, a copy of your estimate is automatically emailed to you AND to us — so both parties have a record.
2

Pick a date from the calendar

The calendar shows our live availability. Dates in red are fully booked. Yellow means 1 slot remaining. We only accept up to 2 bookings per day.

Bookings must be made at least 8 hours before the desired start time. For House Sitting, at least 72 hours notice is required.
3

Select your preferred time

Available time slots run from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Grayed-out slots are either already booked or within the 8-hour advance notice window. Select an open slot to proceed.

Property access reminder:If your unit is inside a condo or building that requires a gate/visitor pass, please arrange this before our arrival. Some properties also restrict contractor access after 5 or 6 PM — confirm with your building management.
4

Choose your cleaning type

Regular Cleaning — sweeping, mopping, dusting, bathroom & kitchen cleaning, general tidying. Price stays as listed.

Deep Cleaning — everything in regular cleaning plus areas often missed: behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout, baseboards, and more. Charged at base price + P20 per sqm. Adjust the sqm slider to your space size.

Post-Construction Cleaning is not currently accepted. We recommend waiting until major dust and debris have fully settled before booking. Thank you for understanding!
5

Select your unit type

Choose the option that best matches your property — Studio, 1 Bedroom, 2 Bedroom, or Commercial. Each includes an estimated duration so you know roughly how long the cleaning will take, and how much of your day you get back.

6

Choose your schedule

Select Initial / One-time if this is a single booking. Choose a recurring schedule (Monthly, Bi-Monthly, Weekly, Bi-Weekly) if you want regular cleaning for the same property.

For recurring bookings: Add your specific dates and times using the "+ Add another date" button. The total upfront downpayment (transport fee × number of sessions) will be shown automatically.
7

Add optional services

Select any additional services you need — extra rooms, bathroom, fridge cleaning, fan cleaning, folding, ironing, and more. Quantities can be adjusted using the + / − buttons.

8

Select your location

Choose the area closest to your property. The transport fee is automatically calculated based on standard rates from our base in Taguig. For areas beyond Taguig, the fee shown is a two-way estimate (to and from your location).

Transport fee is an estimate. The exact amount will be confirmed based on your specific address and actual route before your booking is finalized.
9

Add special instructions (optional)

Tap the quick-select tags if any apply (Kids present, Pet at home, Other workers on-site, Under renovation). Add any detailed instructions in the text box — fragile items, areas to focus on, access codes, etc.

10

Review your estimate & book

Your full estimate is shown on the right panel — itemized charges, estimated total, payment breakdown, and estimated time savings. When ready:

Option A — Book on Messenger: Tapping this button copies your full estimate to clipboard and opens our Messenger chat. Simply paste your details and send.

Option B — Copy Estimate Text: Copy the estimate text to paste anywhere — email, SMS, Viber, etc.

Option C — Save / Print Estimate: Download a PDF copy of your estimate for your records.
11

Wait for booking confirmation

After we receive your request, we'll verify the schedule, confirm the exact transport fee based on your address, and send you the final total with payment details for the reservation fee (downpayment).

Your slot is NOT confirmed until the reservation fee (downpayment = transport fee) has been received and confirmed.
12

Pay the reservation fee

Once you receive our confirmation message with payment details, send the reservation fee via GCash or Maya. Send us a screenshot of the payment and we'll confirm your booking slot officially.

Cancellation policy: Downpayment is non-refundable if cancelled less than 24 hours before the schedule, or if the cleaner is already on the way or onsite. Exception: if booked less than 24 hrs in advance and cancelled at least 4 hrs before start time with cleaner not yet departed — a refund or rebooking may be arranged.
13

We arrive & clean

Our cleaner arrives on time with supplies. We handle everything from top to bottom, following your instructions. When done, we do a walkthrough with you to make sure you're satisfied.

One dedicated cleaner per booking. All services are performed by one professional cleaner — ensuring consistent quality and personal attention to your space.
14

Pay the balance & give feedback

The remaining balance is paid on the day of service. We welcome all feedback — you can use the feedback box in the calculator, message us directly, or leave a review on our Facebook page.

Frequently Asked Questions
How many cleaners will come? ▼
Klaygo PH currently sends one dedicated professional cleaner per booking. This ensures consistent quality, personal attention, and the same trusted cleaner for your recurring bookings.
Is the price estimate final? ▼
The estimate from the calculator is for pricing transparency only and does not confirm a reservation. The final total will be confirmed after we verify your address (for exact transport fee) and send you the payment details.
What's included in Regular vs Deep Cleaning? ▼
Regular Cleaning covers sweeping, mopping, dusting, bathroom & kitchen cleaning, making the bed, and general tidying. Deep Cleaning covers all of that plus areas often missed — behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout lines, baseboards, and more. Deep cleaning is charged at the base price + P20 per sqm of your space.
Do you accept post-construction cleaning? ▼
We currently do not accept post-construction cleaning bookings while renovation activity is ongoing, as heavy dust and debris require specialized equipment. We recommend waiting until major work is fully done and dust has settled before booking. Message us and we'll advise based on your situation.
Do you bring your own cleaning supplies? ▼
Yes! Our cleaner comes with standard cleaning materials. If you have preferred products or specific requirements, please indicate them in the Special Instructions field when booking.
Can I reschedule? ▼
Yes — rescheduling is allowed as long as you notify us at least 24 hours before the scheduled date and time. Message us on Messenger or call 0992.816.4718.
Is house sitting available on weekdays? ▼
Overnight house sitting is currently available on weekends only. It must also be booked at least 72 hours in advance. For weekday inquiries, please message us directly and we'll see what we can arrange.
I manage multiple properties. Is there a discount? ▼
Yes! Airbnb hosts and real estate property managers handling multiple properties may qualify for up to 5% additional discount per confirmed booking. Downpayment for each session must be confirmed before we reserve each slot. Message us to discuss a custom arrangement.

Estimate ID System

Every time a customer selects a time slot, an Estimate ID is automatically generated:

KGPH-EST-YYYYMMDD-###

KGPH = KlayGoPH identifier
EST = Estimate
YYYYMMDD = Date generated
### = Sequential number (resets per device)

When a booking is confirmed and invoice is needed, the series changes to: KGPH-INV-YYYYMMDD-###

How to Block Dates on the Calendar

1.
Go to calendar.google.com and sign in to klaygoph@gmail.com
2.
Click the date and time you want to block (e.g. May 15, 10:00 AM)
3.
Make sure to add it under KLAYGO PH - Bookings calendar (not your personal calendar)
4.
Set exact start and end times — NOT "all day." The booking app reads specific times.
5.
Set visibility to Public so the app can read it
6.
Save. Click "Refresh Calendar" on the booking page to see the update immediately.
