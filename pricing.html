📋 Booking Policies

📋 Pricing Transparency ▼
This calculator is for pricing transparency only.
It does not confirm a reservation. Booking is confirmed only after the reservation fee has been received and confirmed.
🔄 Reschedule Policy ▼
Rescheduling is allowed with at least 24 hours notice before your scheduled cleaning.
🚫 Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy ▼
Free Cancellation or Rescheduling
You may cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your scheduled service with no charge.

Late Cancellation (Less than 24 Hours)
Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled service will forfeit the reservation fee already paid. No refund will be issued.

Same-Day Bookings
Same-day bookings are subject to availability and must be confirmed by our team. A 50% reservation fee is required upon confirmation. Once confirmed and the team assigned, cancellations are not eligible for a refund. If the cleaner is already dispatched, the booking is treated as a late cancellation. We recommend booking in advance.

Same-Day Cancellation / No-Show
If a booking is cancelled on the day of service, or if our team cannot proceed due to no property access, incorrect address, client unavailability, or no-show — the reservation fee is non-refundable.

Exceptions
Fees may be waived for verified medical emergencies, severe weather conditions, or unexpected urgent situations — reviewed fairly on a case-by-case basis.

Why this policy exists: When a booking is confirmed, we reserve that time specifically for you and assign a cleaning professional. Last-minute changes affect schedules, cleaner income stability, and other clients waiting for availability.
🏠 Large Spaces (90+ sqm) ▼
An additional cleaner will be assigned to ensure quality and efficiency. This may affect scheduling — we'll confirm details upon booking.

For 90+ sqm spaces: A 50% reservation fee is required to secure your reservation. This applies for weekend, same-day, and holiday bookings as well.
💳 Reservation Fee & Payment ▼
The reservation fee secures your slot. For standard bookings: reservation fee = whichever is higher between 30% of your total estimate or the transport fee. For same-day, weekend, holiday, or 90+ sqm bookings: 50% of the total estimate is required.

Accepted: GCash · Bank Transfer. Payment details are sent after we confirm your booking via Messenger.
🧹 Active On-Site Work ▼
We do not service properties where active third-party workers, ongoing renovation, or construction is taking place. Please rebook once the space is ready for cleaning.
📌 Booking Agreement
By sending us this estimate and completing your reservation fee, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to all of the booking policies listed above.
