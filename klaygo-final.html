Cleaning Type

Tap to select and see what's included

Regular Cleaning
Base price
Sweeping, mopping, dusting surfaces, bathroom & kitchen cleaning, making bed, general tidying. Perfect for routine upkeep of your space.
Not included:

Inside cabinets, behind appliances, grout scrubbing, window glass exterior, laundry, or deep-level cleaning.

Deep Cleaning
See estimate
Everything in Regular Cleaning, plus: inside cabinets & drawers, behind & underneath appliances, grout scrubbing, baseboards, light fixtures, and hard-to-reach corners. Ideal for spaces that haven't been cleaned in a while.
Not included:

Window glass exterior, laundry & ironing (available as add-ons), post-construction debris, or hazardous material handling.

Move In / Move Out
See estimate
Comprehensive cleaning for properties being vacated or newly occupied. Covers all Regular Cleaning areas plus inside cabinets & drawers, appliance interiors, hard-to-reach corners — leaving the space fresh and ready for the next occupant.
Not included:

Removal of large furniture or junk, pest control, painting, repairs, window glass exterior, or post-construction debris.

Post-Construction Cleaning — Not Currently Accepted We recommend waiting until major dust and debris have fully settled before booking. Thank you for your understanding!

Booking Policies

-
This calculator is for pricing transparency only. It does not confirm a reservation. Booking is confirmed only after the reservation fee (downpayment = transport fee) has been received and confirmed.
-
Reschedule: Allowed with at least 24 hours notice.
-
Cancellation: Downpayment is non-refundable if cancelled less than 24 hrs before, or if the cleaner is already on the way or onsite.
-
Exception: If booked less than 24 hrs in advance and you cancel at least 4 hrs before with cleaner not yet departed — a refund or rebooking may be arranged.
-
Large Spaces (90+ sqm): An additional cleaner will be assigned to ensure quality and efficiency. This may affect scheduling — we'll confirm details upon booking.
-
Larger Spaces (90+ sqm) & Weekend Slots: A 50% downpayment is required to secure and confirm your booking reservation.
